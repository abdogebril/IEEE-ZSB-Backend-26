## 1️⃣ Difference between DBMS and RDBMS

- **DBMS (Database Management System):**  
A general system for managing data. It can store and manage data without enforcing strong relationships between tables.

- **RDBMS (Relational Database Management System):**  
A system that manages relational databases. It supports tables linked by primary and foreign keys and maintains relationships between data.

--------------------------------------------------

## 2️⃣ Difference between DDL and DML

- **DDL (Data Definition Language):**  
Used to define and manage the structure of database objects, such as creating, altering, and deleting tables.

- **DML (Data Manipulation Language):**  
Used to manipulate the data inside tables, such as inserting, updating, deleting, and retrieving data.

---------------------------------------------------

## 3️⃣ Why is Normalization important for a large system like a university database?

- Reduces data redundancy.  
- Prevents inconsistencies and errors in the data.  
- Makes it easier to update and maintain data.  
- Improves performance and keeps the database organized and stable.
